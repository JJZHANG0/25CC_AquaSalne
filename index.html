Key Innovation

Physical-Driven Anti-Scaling Technology

Our breakthrough buoyancy-sensing system monitors brine concentration in real-time, automatically discharging high-concentration saltwater before crystallization occurs. This preventive approach eliminates scaling damage and extends device lifespan without costly maintenance.
