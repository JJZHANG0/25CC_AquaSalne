Our Journey

From Idea to Impact

Tracking the key milestones in AutoSaline's development and future roadmap.

Early 2025

Project Inception

Five middle school students discover that over 2 billion people lack safe water access and commit to finding a solution.

November 2025

Breakthrough Innovation

Complete core theoretical modeling of the buoyancy-driven float recovery solution and conduct successful simplified tests.

November-December 2025

Laboratory Validation

Achieve 95% discharge success rate over 24 test cycles with zero salt crystallization in simulated seawater environment.

December 2025

First Prototype Complete

Build fully functional prototype with 2kWh energy storage, producing stable 6.5L daily output over 30 days fault-free operation.

Q1 2026

Funding Round

Seeking $150,000 seed funding for product testing, certifications, and manufacturing setup.

Q2-Q3 2026

Product Certification & Tooling

Complete safety certifications and establish manufacturing partnerships for initial production.

Q4 2026

Pilot Deployment

Launch pilot programs with select coastal communities and government partners to validate real-world performance.

2027

Market Launch

Full commercial launch through e-commerce platforms and distribution partnerships, targeting initial sales of 1,000+ units.
