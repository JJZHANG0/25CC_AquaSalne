Our Story

From School Project to Global Solution

01

The Discovery

Five middle school students discovered through research that over 2 billion people lack safe water access, with coastal communities particularly vulnerable due to lack of infrastructure and maintenance services.

02

The Commitment

Motivated by this global challenge, we committed to developing an off-grid, low-maintenance desalination solution that could operate autonomously in harsh coastal environments without skilled technicians.

03

The Innovation

After months of research and testing, we developed a breakthrough physical-driven brine-flush mechanism that prevents salt crystallization, enabling reliable long-term operation where traditional systems fail.

Our Solution

AutoSaline: Autonomous Water for All

AutoSaline is a compact, solar-powered seawater desalination device specifically designed for remote coastal households, small businesses, and governments facing freshwater scarcity.
