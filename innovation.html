Innovation

Breakthrough Desalination Technology

Our physical-driven anti-scaling system fundamentally solves the maintenance challenges that have limited small-scale desalination for decades.

01

Buoyancy-Sensing System

Four precision buoyancy switches calibrated to 1.2 g/cm³ density monitor brine concentration in real-time, acting as the 'taste buds' of the device.

02

Preventive Brine Discharge

When concentration reaches critical levels, corrosion-resistant peristaltic pumps automatically discharge high-concentration brine before crystallization occurs.

03

Solar-Thermal Integration

Fresnel lens and photovoltaic panels work together - one for heat generation, one for electricity, with phase-change material cold storage for 24/7 operation.

04

Floatable Modular Design

The system can float on seawater and multiple units can be linked together, enabling scalable deployment from single households to small communities.
